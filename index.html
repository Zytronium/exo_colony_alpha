About the Colony

Exo Colony Alpha is humanity's first interstellar settlement. Located in the Alpha Centauri system, this colony represents a bold step forward inexploration, innovation, and survival beyond Earth. The first interstellar colony ships are planned to launch by late 2035, and you can be on one of them! Equipped with microscopic cameras, tiny interstellar probes propelled by next-generation solar sails have been sent to Alpha Centauri's Proxima B, the first planet orbiting Proxima Centauri, one of the three stars in the Alpha Centauri system. These probes have taken photos of the planet and its landscape, which have been colorized and enhanced. One of them is displayed above.

Take on the challenge of surviving and thriving at Exo Colony Alpha under three suns painting an ever-shifting sky of red and gold! Help the colony overcome the many challenges to come. Harsh radiation, unpredictable weather patterns, and other unforeseen challenges will test the limits of human ingenuity. However, together, we will overcome, bringing about a better future for the descendants of the colony and the rest of humanity as a whole.

This is more than survival. It is the beginning of a new era. With cutting-edge technology, terraforming research, and the indomitable human spirit, we will transform Proxima b into a home for future generations. Join the pioneers, shape the destiny of the colony, and ensure that humanity thrives among the stars!
